Download Zoom on a computer:

1. Go to the Zoom website

2. Click Download under Zoom Client for Meetings

3. Save it to your desktop

4. Wait for Zoom to install

5. You have succesfully installed Zoom on your Computer

Download Zoom on the app store:

1. Click on your app store

2. Type in Zoom

3. Click Install
